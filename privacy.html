Paragon Systems™ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Paragon Systems™ ("the Bot," "we," "our," or "us"), the official Discord bot of Paragon eSports, collects, uses, stores, and protects information obtained through your use of the Bot.

By using Paragon Systems™, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Paragon Systems™ may collect and process the following information:

  • Discord User IDs;
  • Discord Server IDs;
  • Discord Channel IDs;
  • Verification and onboarding information;
  • XP, leveling, and progression data;
  • Looking for Team (LFT) listings and related information;
  • Server configuration settings;
  • Command usage information;
  • Diagnostic and error logs required for maintenance and security.

We do not intentionally collect passwords, payment information, government identification numbers, or other highly sensitive personal information through the Bot.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected may be used to:

  • Operate and provide Bot features;
  • Manage verification and onboarding systems;
  • Maintain XP and leveling functionality;
  • Facilitate Looking for Team services;
  • Provide tournament, event, and notification features;
  • Maintain server-specific settings;
  • Diagnose and resolve technical issues;
  • Protect the Bot against abuse, spam, and malicious activity;
  • Improve existing features and develop new ones.

3. Data Sharing

Paragon eSports does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Information may only be disclosed:

  • When required by applicable law or legal process;
  • To protect the security, rights, or property of Paragon eSports;
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, or malicious activity;
  • When necessary to provide services through trusted third-party providers.

4. Third-Party Services

Paragon Systems™ may integrate with third-party services, including:

  • Discord;
  • Streaming platforms;
  • Game-related APIs;
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers.

These services operate under their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices.

5. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide Bot functionality;
  • Maintain user progression systems;
  • Ensure server configurations remain operational;
  • Resolve disputes and investigate abuse;
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Data that is no longer required may be deleted or anonymized.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your information, including the right to:

  • Request access to information associated with you;
  • Request correction of inaccurate information;
  • Request deletion of information where applicable;
  • Object to certain processing activities;
  • Withdraw consent where consent is the legal basis for processing.

Requests may be submitted through the official Paragon eSports Discord server.

8. Children's Privacy

Paragon Systems™ is not directed toward children under the minimum age required to use Discord in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect information from children in violation of applicable law.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Continued use of Paragon Systems™ after updates become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Information

Questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed to the Paragon eSports staff through the official Paragon eSports Discord server.

By using Paragon Systems™, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
